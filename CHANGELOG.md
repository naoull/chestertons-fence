## [1.0.0] — 2026-05-05

### Added

- Initial public release.
- `chestertons-fence` skill that forces a 6-step workflow (STOP → Identify → Investigate → Hypothesize → Decide → Document) before any deletion or refactor.
- Stack-agnostic guidance covering frontend, backend, infrastructure, migrations, IaC, and CI.
- Recommended catalog template for per-repo fence inventories.
- Anti-patterns and pre-PR checklist baked into the skill.
